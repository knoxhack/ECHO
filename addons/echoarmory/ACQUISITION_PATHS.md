## Intentional Depot-Only Gear

These are intentionally recipe-less in v1 and should be obtained through faction-gated Logistics depot offers when the sibling systems are present:

- `veil_sabre`
- `energy_rifle`
- `convergence_gun`
- `construct_gauntlet`
- `veil_resistant_helm`
- `drone_leggings`
- `construct_harness`

If faction or Logistics data is unavailable, creative users can inspect these items for testing. Survival users keep the item if acquired, but use, protection, station upgrades, and Terminal equip actions remain locked with a visible message.
